Simple SF Energy Sharing ABM

Simulation time is explicitly 2025-01-01 00:00 through 2025-01-30 23:00, with no-sun disturbance on 2025-01-15.

Agent state: A_i(t) = {health_i(t), generation_i(t), demand_i(t), storage_i(t), generosity}.

Healthy balance: (generation + starting_storage - demand) + (energy_received - energy_exported). A building is alive when this value is at least 0.

Energy sharing rule: gift = min(surplus, energy_request) * generosity.

Only two performance metrics are reported: % building alive and resilience. Resilience is normalized area under the performance curve: R = integral Q(t) dt / integral Q0 dt, where Q(t) = % building alive and Q0 = 100%.
